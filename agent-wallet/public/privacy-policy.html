Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

AgentPay, Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our AI commerce wallet platform ("Service").

By using AgentPay, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Financial Information: Credit card details (processed by Stripe), transaction history, wallet balances
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID, address verification (when required)
  • Communication Data: Support tickets, emails, chat messages

2.2 Technical Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
  • AI Agent Data: Agent configurations, spending limits, transaction patterns
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, analytics cookies, preference cookies

2.3 Third-Party Integration Data

  • AI Platform Data: Interactions with ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI platforms
  • Purchase Data: Items bought, services used, merchant information
  • API Usage: Frequency and patterns of AI agent transactions

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Process payments and maintain wallet balances
  • Enable AI agent functionality and autonomous transactions
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Send transaction confirmations and account updates

3.2 Security and Compliance

  • Prevent fraud, money laundering, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with financial regulations and reporting requirements
  • Monitor for suspicious activities and security threats
  • Verify identity and perform due diligence checks

3.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Optimize AI agent performance and reliability
  • Conduct research and analytics (in aggregated, anonymized form)

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties who help us operate our platform:

  • Stripe: Payment processing and financial services
  • Cloud Providers: Data hosting and storage (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics: Service usage analysis and improvement
  • Security: Fraud detection and prevention services

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • In response to lawful requests from government authorities
  • To comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
  • For tax reporting and regulatory compliance
  • To protect our rights, safety, and property

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new owner as part of the business transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to personal information
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
  • Compliance: PCI DSS compliance for payment card data
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and penetration testing

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Portability

  • Request copies of your personal information
  • Export your transaction history and account data
  • Access AI agent configurations and activity logs

6.2 Data Correction and Deletion

  • Update or correct your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

6.3 Privacy Controls

  • Manage cookie preferences and tracking settings
  • Control notification preferences
  • Set data sharing preferences for analytics

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-group transfers

8. Retention Period

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements (typically 7 years for financial records)
  • Resolve disputes and prevent fraud
  • Improve our services through anonymized analytics

9. Children's Privacy

AgentPay is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

10. Regional Privacy Rights

10.1 European Union (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to rectification, erasure, and restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing and automated decision-making
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

10.2 California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Delete personal information (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Enable core functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how users interact with our platform
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements (with your consent)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences or our cookie management tool.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our platform. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last modified.
