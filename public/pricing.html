Frequently asked questions

How does Aslan compare to Stripe for small agents?

For small agents (750-3000 transactions/month), Aslan costs ~$0.01-0.04 per transaction vs Stripe's ~$0.30-0.82. You get 750 FREE transactions monthly with Sandbox, then just $29/month for 3,000 more. AI-specific controls, spending limits, and multi-agent management that Stripe doesn't offer.

What counts as a successful authorization?

A successful authorization is when your AI agent requests permission to spend and our system approves it based on your spending controls. Failed authorizations (due to limits, restrictions, etc.) don't count against your quota.

How does overage pricing work?

If you exceed your monthly authorization quota, you'll be charged the per-authorization rate ($0.02-0.05 depending on plan). Much cheaper than traditional payment processing for AI agents with frequent small transactions.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments. Start with Sandbox (free), upgrade to Starter ($19), then Builder ($79) as you grow.

Is there a free trial?

The Sandbox plan is free forever with 750 authorizations per month - enough for real projects! For paid plans, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. Perfect for testing your AI agents before committing.

Why choose Aslan over traditional payment processors?

AI agents make frequent micro-authorizations that traditional processors charge $0.30+ each for. Aslan is designed for AI workflows with bulk authorization pricing, intelligent spending controls, and multi-agent management - all at a fraction of the cost.
