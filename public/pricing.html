Frequently asked questions

What counts as a successful authorization?

A successful authorization is when your AI agent requests permission to spend and our system approves it based on your spending controls. Failed authorizations (due to limits, restrictions, etc.) don't count against your quota.

How does overage pricing work?

If you exceed your monthly authorization quota, you'll be charged the per-authorization rate for each additional successful authorization. No surprises - you can set spending alerts and limits.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.

Is there a free trial?

The Sandbox plan is free forever with 50 authorizations per month. For paid plans, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and can arrange invoicing for Enterprise customers. All plans include secure payment processing for your AI agents.
